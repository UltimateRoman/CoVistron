CoVistron

Presented by Beta_Codinators

An all-in-one project to curb the pandemic by guiding the quarantined people

The World is affected by the CoViD-19 pandemic and many nations have put their people under lockdown. Each individual has the duty to follow the guidelines set by their respective health organisations to curb the pandemic. But home-quarantine can be an unpleasant experience for many, especially for long periods of time. This can take a toll on people and may cause unprecedented consequences. To make quarantine a safe and satisfactory affair, we have devised CoVistron.



CoVistron consists of a cluster of intelligent devices(to help with basic health precautions in daily life activities) with home-automation(to minimise contact with home appliances and surfaces, all coordinated by the Web App to guide, accompany and entertain people under quarantine.

Please login for Home-Automation

In addition to home-automation, we offer the following devices...

1. Smart Cap - For the people who have trouble trying not to touch their face. It can preform an incredible role and prevent you from getting infected. It is equipped with an ultrasonic sensor to detect whether your hands are approaching your face and warns you using a buzzer alert.



2. Handwashing tracker - Makes you wash your hands for atleast 20 sec. Handwashing for atleast 20 sec with a suitable soap or handwash helps to cripple any microbes which may have made it to your hands. A sensor checks your time period of your handwash and the buzzer beeps continuously if you didn't make it to the 20 second threshold.



3. Contactless handwash/sanitiser dispenser - Prevents spread of infection from shared handwashes/sanitisers. A simple motor helps to deliver suitable amount of sanitiser/handwash to your hands by detecting the presence of your hands.
