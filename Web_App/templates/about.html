About CoVistron

How it helps quarantined people

CoVistron aims to curb the spread of CoVid-19 by being a companion for home-quarantined people around the world. It brings with itself a cluster of intelligent devices powered by IoT to fulfill this purpose. These devices help people reinforce basic but necessary precautions such as washing hands for atleast 20 seconds, avoiding touching the face, minimizing contact around the home, etc. CoVistron deploys home automation to minimize physical contact of people with home appliances.

CoVistron's Web App boasts of a real-time functional CoVid-19 tracker giving interested users data to analyse the current situation. However we recommend you take its definiteness with a grain of salt.

We have listed general precautions in the Web App to promote awareness. Also we request all of you to follow the guidelines set by the WHO and the health ministries of your respective countries without fail.

CoVistron's Web App also has an amusing section 'Feeling Bored?' to help people find thrilling methods to entertain themselves in case their enthusiasm has become depleted.
